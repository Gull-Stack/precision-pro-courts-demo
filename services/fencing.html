Professional Court Fencing

A great court deserves great fencing. Our professional fencing solutions keep balls in play, define your court boundaries, enhance privacy, and add a polished, professional look to any installation.

We offer a range of fencing styles, heights, and finishes to match your court design, property aesthetics, and budget. Every installation is built to withstand Utah's weather and years of heavy use.

  • Chain-link fencing (galvanized & vinyl-coated)
  • Multiple height options: 6', 8', 10', 12'
  • Windscreen & privacy mesh options
  • Custom gates & access points
  • Color options to match your court
  • Commercial-grade materials & posts
